A Safe Space for Neurodivergent Adults

Zebra Stripes is a nonprofit organization dedicated to building neurodivergent-informed environments and systems that support adults, especially those diagnosed later in life. We create real-world infastructure that centers regulation, autonomy, and sustainable function.

Primary Audience

Zebra Stripes serves neurodivergent adults,

age 18 and over,

who are navigating diagnosis burnout,

loss of function, or identity collapse

after years of masking.

Long-Term Vision

Zebra Stripes is a structural intervention.

It exists not only to support the people who fell through the cracks,

but to rewire the systems that created those cracks in the first place.

We aren't here to heal in isolation. We're here to REBUILD access!
